Best of Resume's Albums(Deluxe version)

编辑 由 Resume 2017-04-03创建，共124个应用

First anniversary of me making albums in Coolapk.
总结过去的应用集纪念本人在酷安做应用集一周年(可能是本应用集唯一一句中文)
To the days when we still believe in purity and civility.
To the days when we still believe we can succeed where other people can not.

  • 标准通

    The very first thing I told every Coolapker about myself is that I major in mechanical Science, which sounds much more boring than it really is. But that is beside the point. I am only stating my major because I want to tell you guys that it gave me an interesting perspective of Apps, that is, I want apps to be at least a little bit useful.

  • 机械设计手册

    ╮(╯▽╰)╭ Must-have app for someone trying to become a mechanical engineer.

  • eDrawings

    Solidworks files viewed on Android Platform. 1.99 US Dollars Recommended for mechanical enngineers(or anyone trying to be one) who use SW a lot

  • CAD迷你看图

    Best dwg viewer I have ever used. Faster than "CAD快速看图". A free dwg a day, keeps you happy all day.

  • 中望CAD Touch

    Edit and save dwg on the go. Some of you may be expecting AutoCAD360, but I cannot use it without registering an account.

  • 3D PDF Reader

    Supported file types: obj, u3d, pdf(3d), stl, hsf, prc Comes in handy when you want to show your cad files with your coworkers

  • ForceEffect

    Draw a few rods, forces, and pivot points, and you will get magnitudes of reaction forces and diagrams of force and torque.

  • 甘特侠

    Gantt Chart easily edited on your phone. Though mostly we use Excel on PC to do this. (For your information, Gantt Chart is used to do project planning.)

  • 声声-无需付费下载,可搜各平台音乐

    A place where Intellectual Property Rights of musicians goes to hell and piracy rules. Sadly popular and useful.

  • MaterialUp

    Now it is time to make everything material.(ˉ▽￣～) Elegant MD everywhere.

  • ExView - 二次元ACGN多功能阅览平台

    Di di di di.... Old driver coming through(ˉ▽￣～)

  • Google Spotlight Stories

    Short stories in VR. trilling or funny, cute or scary, intense or inspiring.

  • Mathematics计算器

    No doubt this is my favorite calculator. Such a small app with so many features! Complex number operations, matrix operations, Statistical calculations, and so on, all in one.

  • META计算器

    Second favorite calculator. Most amazing feature: Coding in Lua

  • 就看天气

    Only weather. Say no to news, no to ads, no to anything else! However, it provides less information than “天气家”

  • 天气家

    Sufficient information. Gorgeous UI Hardly any defects. Sometimes the weather forcast is not quite correct.

  • 照片编辑器

    pandecheng loves this. We love pandecheng. So we love this(o_ _)ﾉ

  • 文献云阅读

    Produced by Kexuehome. Journal abstracts are available. Website:http://m.kexuehome.com/sci/ Subscribe to get instant updates on journals of your concern. Tons of journals, right at your fingertip.

  • 语记

    Single best speech-to-text app I've ever used in China. Supports a variety of dialects. A nice substitute for Youdao Note or Evernote.

  • LaTeX编辑器

    Formula and expressions displayed in a proper way. Now you may not need a PC to work on your essays.

  • 拍照识别翻译

    Photo editing is less convenient than CamScanner, but has a better recognition ability, that is, a higher correct rate.

  • QPython

    Py on your phone with Coolapk. Python on your phone with Qpython.(This app supports only python 2.x. If you wish to run python 3.x scripts, then the other app, Qpython3, should do.)

  • 奥维互动地图

    Ugly interface..... But great utility. Map data from numerous sources with sufficient details.

  • Solid Explorer 2

    MD With certain add-ons, it would support ftp and otg.

  • 数码音乐厅

    Always wanna go but first I need to fill my empty pocket.

  • 经济学人影片

    The Economist films provides high-quality films about the world, not always about economy. There are short videos in "Daily watch" column and long ones organised and published in series. You can work on your UK accent with it.(though I personally prefer US accent)

  • 生命的故事

    Story of Life. The name of the app pretty much explains what its content is about. Clips of great nature brought to you by BBC Earth.

  • 万门大学

    Learn languages or economy or managementԅ(¯﹃¯ԅ)

  • GitSkarios

    My fav Third party Github Client. Perfect in every way.

  • 超理论坛 for Android

    Arts students out. A forum for Science students where numbers and serious logics fly.

  • 经济学人
